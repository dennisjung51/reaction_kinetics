About this simulation

This app simulates a consecutive reaction mechanism:

A → B → C

Where:

  • A is the starting reactant (green line)
  • B is an intermediate species (blue line)
  • C is the final product (red line)

The concentration profiles are governed by:

  • k₁: Rate constant for conversion of A to B
  • k₂: Rate constant for conversion of B to C

Key observations:

  • When k₁ > k₂: B accumulates (slower second step)
  • When k₁ < k₂: B forms and depletes quickly (faster second step)
  • When k₁ ≈ k₂: Intermediate concentration profile for B
